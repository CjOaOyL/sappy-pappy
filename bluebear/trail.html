💡 Trail Tips

👟Wear trail shoes or boots — the descent section can be slippery when wet.
💧Bring water. There's a seasonal stream at the valley floor but it's not safe to drink from.
🌿Best in spring and fall. Summer foliage is dense and beautiful; winter snow makes it magical with microspikes.
🐾Dogs welcome on leash. Wildlife is active at dawn and dusk — you may spot deer or wild turkey.
📵Cell service is limited on property. Download an offline map before heading out.
🎒Trail map and orientation are provided at the cottage on arrival.

🏡 Getting to the Trailhead

1️⃣The trail begins at the cottage. Step off the back deck and follow the orange blazes into the tree line.
2️⃣Bear left at the first fork to do the loop clockwise (recommended — descent first).
3️⃣The full loop returns to within 100 yards of where you started. The cottage will be visible through the trees.
