Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the things guests ask us most. Don't see your question? Use the inquiry form above.

Is the property kid-friendly?

▼
Absolutely — this is a place built with families in mind. Kids love the open grounds for exploring, the natural slopes for sledding in winter, and the blueberry meadow in summer. The property has ample outdoor space to roam, and the cottage itself is cozy and welcoming. We do have natural terrain (woods, slopes, uneven ground), so normal outdoor supervision applies. Little ones will leave happily worn out.

Are pets allowed?

▼
Please check the current listing on Airbnb for the most up-to-date pet policy, as this can change seasonally. The property has abundant outdoor space that dogs tend to absolutely love — 80 acres of woods and meadow is a serious upgrade for any pup. If you plan to bring a pet, please mention it in your inquiry so we can make sure everything is set up for a comfortable stay.

What is the cancellation policy?

▼
Cancellation terms are managed through the Airbnb platform and are clearly listed on the booking page before you confirm your reservation. We recommend reviewing those terms at the time of booking. If life happens and you need to adjust your dates, reach out early — we always try to work with guests where we can, within the platform's policies.

How far is the nearest grocery store?

▼
There are grocery options in the nearby towns of Hadley and Corinth (about 10–15 minutes away) for basics and supplies. For a fuller selection, Saratoga Springs is about 30 minutes and has a wide range of grocery stores, specialty markets, and restaurants. We recommend stocking up before you arrive so you can settle in and stay as long as you want without running back into town.

Is there WiFi at the cottage?

▼
Yes, WiFi is available at the cottage. That said, you're in the Adirondacks — we gently encourage you to use it for streaming a movie on a rainy evening rather than recreating your home office. The property is a place to be present. The stars don't care about your inbox, and neither should you — at least for a few days.

What is the check-in process?

▼
Check-in details, access codes, and arrival instructions are provided through the Airbnb platform once your booking is confirmed. We make the process as smooth as possible — most guests are inside the cottage and comfortable within a few minutes of arriving. If you anticipate a late arrival or have any special needs, just message us through Airbnb ahead of time and we'll make sure everything is arranged.
